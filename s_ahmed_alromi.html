Ahmed Alromi

I am half costarican and half german. I studied education and was always fascinated by Costa Ricas cultural and natural diversity. Additionally I lived in different countries throughout my lifetime and got to meet different cultures. This unforgettable rich experience has encouraged me to show travelers this beautiful country.

What places and activities do you specialize in?

"Family Experiences Honeymoons Active Travel (hiking,biking, rafting) Self-drive adventure packages Luxury Trips Wellness & Yoga Sustainble Tourism Cultural Encounters"

How did you get involved in travel?

I got the chance to start working as a freelance trilingual naturalist guide in Costa Rica and have had the privilige to help groups, families & couples in their travel planning as well as assisting them in person. My work has not only brought me to get to meet great travelers and guide them. It has additionally given me the expertise to get to know almost every little spot of Costa Rica with it`s rich diversity. I invite you to explore an unforgettable experience with me visiting active volcanoes, pristine beaches, diverse wildlife, imposing cloud- and rainforests and other natural wonders as well as getting to meet people with a warm heart.

Please share a unique travel experience you will never forget.

I could not mention a specific travel experience I will never forget due to the fact that there are so many unique moments I've had the possibility to experience. Our beautiful country offers the possibility to see 7 of the most important animals of the American Continent and some say even of the world. I have seen a Jaguar, Puma, Humpback Whales, the holy bird of the Mayas the Quetzal, Tapirs & the biggest turtle of the world the leatherback in unforeseen, unforgettable, and surprising moments.
